Who We Are

EcoVoyage was founded in 2020 by a group of environmental scientists and travel enthusiasts who saw the devastating effects of mass tourism and decided to create a better way.

Our vision is a world where travel actively restores ecosystems rather than depletes them. We meticulously vet every destination, tour operator, and accommodation to ensure they meet our rigorous 50-point sustainability criteria.

Our Core Values

  • Transparency: We show you exactly where your money goes.
  • Conservation: 5% of all profits go directly to rewilding projects.
  • Community: We empower local populations and indigenous groups.
  • Zero-Carbon: We calculate and offset 100% of the emissions from every trip booked through our platform.
