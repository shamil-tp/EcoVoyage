Overview

Costa Rica is a global leader in conservation, with over 25% of its land protected. Visitors can experience cloud forests, volcanoes, and pristine beaches while supporting local eco-initiatives. It is essential to approach this environment with respect, leaving no trace and supporting the local economy. We partner with indigenous communities to ensure that tourism dollars directly benefit conservation efforts.

Wildlife & Conservation

This region is home to countless endangered species. By visiting through our verified eco-partners, your park fees directly fund anti-poaching units, habitat restoration, and biological research.

Sustainable Activities

  • Guided nature walks with local biologists
  • Non-motorized water sports (kayaking, paddleboarding)
  • Tree planting volunteering sessions
  • Cultural exchange programs
