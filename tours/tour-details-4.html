Tour Overview

Join us on an unforgettable 4-day journey that balances thrilling adventure with deep respect for the environment. Our "leave no trace" policy ensures that your footprint is minimal, while your impact on the local economy is positive.

Itinerary highlights

Meet your guides, settle into your sustainable accommodation, and learn about the local conservation efforts we will support during the tour.

Engage in zero-emission activities like hiking, kayaking, and wildlife spotting. All meals are locally sourced and organic.

Participate in a local community project (e.g. tree planting) before heading home with incredible memories and a zero-carbon footprint.

What's Included

  • Carbon offset for your entire trip
  • All eco-friendly accommodations
  • Locally sourced organic meals
  • Expert naturalist guides
